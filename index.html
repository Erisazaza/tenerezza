About Us

«Tenerezza» lifestyle concept, mostly focused on flowers but not only. «Tenerezza» takes its inspiration from the beauty of wild nature and classic still life paintings. We also totally passionate about catering our designs to work in harmony with your interiors, your lifestyle. Be it for bouquets, brands, events, weddings. And because good things must share, we also specialize in conducting flower workshops.

History

Aude is the founder of “Tenerezza” lifestyle concept, mostly focused on flowers but not only. Tenerezza – Flowers takes its inspiration from the beauty of wild nature and classic still life paintings.

“Flowers came into my life without notice. My interest in nature grew stronger in 2015 when I arrived in Singapore, the lush greenery stimulated it, my passion for all things floral blossomed here in the Lion city.”

I am totally passionate about catering my designs to work in harmony with your interiors, your lifestyle. Be it for bouquets, brands, events, weddings.
